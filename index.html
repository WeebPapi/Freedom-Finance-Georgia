Our Mission

Freedom Finance Georgia is a limited company which serves the mission of spreading and elevating the level of financial literacy in Georgian teenagers, at Freedom Finance we believe that strong financial education will result in generating the ability of making more well-informed and rational decisions about personal finance, investments, budgeting, lending and borrowing, we believe that strong financial literacy can put you far ahead in life. At Freedom Finance, we strive to provide quality information and education and help the teenagers who are about to step into real life attain financial literacy to make more informed decisions about their finance. Our company was founded by two teenagers who were given the opportunity to study in England and now are motivated to share and give back to the Georgian community.
